{: .lead}

But it can be hard to know where to start. 
{: .lead}



## The Sketchbook
The AI Sketchbook is Amaranth's scratch pad of how people are using AI in different teaching and research contexts. 

These are not best practices. Not polished success stories. Not detailed tutorials. Just some skeetches of what was tried, what the AI produced, what worked, what didn't, and what questions it raised.

**Teaching sketches** document assignments and classroom setups where AI becomes a thinking partner as well as an object of critical inquiry — where students learn something about how knowledge gets made, evaluated, and trusted, not just how to produce text faster.

**Research sketches** suggest some uses for AI in common research tasks: what it actually took to analyze a document collection, build an interactive map, or generate a 3D model from archival drawings.

## Contribute a sketch
The power of the sketchbook is in its diversity and stems from a collaborative spirit. If you've tried something with AI in a class or research project at UNM, we hope you'll considering sharing your experience. Remember these are just sketches, not polished tutorials.
